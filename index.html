About Me

Heyy! I’m Tanishi Tyagi—born in Pune, Maharashtra, originally from Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, but currently living in Faridabad (basically, I’m from everywhere at this point).

I’m a Dual Degree student in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad, trying my best to make sense of code while wondering why semicolons exist. I’m not exceptionally good at coding (yet!), but hey, I’m improving daily, and that’s what matters, right?

When I’m not staring at my laptop, you’ll probably find me dancing somewhere (on stage, in my room, or in my head). I’ve been a choreographer for TDC (IIIT-H’s Dance Club), and honestly, dancing is my favorite kind of cardio. I also love traveling, whether it’s exploring new cities or just finding the best street food spots.

Currently, I’m working on an IoT-based blind stick and a tech-medical startup (which makes me sound smarter than I feel). But at the end of the day, I’m just someone who loves creating, learning, and vibing through life!
